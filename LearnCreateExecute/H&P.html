Learn HTML Head & Page Title






The head and page title are where your html document ususally starts with.

The <head> tag is seen in between the <html> tag and before the <body> tag in an HTML Document.


Head and Page Title




Head:

The element <head> is the container of metadata (data about data).

This element is placed between the <html> element and the <body> element.



Page Title:

In every web, there should be a Page Title: <title>.

The Page Title is placed inside the <head> tag
